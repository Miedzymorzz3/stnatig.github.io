My Journey as a Tour Guide

About Me

Dear Reader,

I’m Natig, or Fred if you prefer my English nickname. My heart has always yearned for exploration, captivated by the beauty and complexity of the world. This realization led me to my calling: to guide others on their journeys, helping them uncover the hidden magic each destination holds. I find solace in being a giant with shoulders for people to sit on, just as Newton once said.

How It All Began

My adventure began with a transformative trip to Georgia when I was a kid. The rich history and warmth of the breeze in the beach stirred something deep within me. I felt a void in my life before discovering my actual purpose until before becoming a guide, and ever since, I’ve been on a quest to share that same magic with others.

Starting out as a local guide was intimidating. However, with each tour, I found joy in sharing stories and insights. To date of September 27th 2024, I’ve guided over 500 tourists across more than 15 countries. Each encounter has shaped me, and while I’ve shared countless smiles with tourists, I felt that I really did a good job at the end of the day.

My Philosophy

Guiding isn’t just a job; it’s a heartfelt commitment to creating connections between places and nature with visitors. Travel is about sharing experiences, embracing the unknown, and learning from one another. As we wander through serene landscapes or busy streets, I aim to help you feel the vibes of each place we explore during our trips, even after.

I take pride in tailoring each tour to fit your unique interests. Whether you seek history, nature, or a blend of both, my goal is to create an experience that resonates with your own preference.

Together, we’ll embark on an adventure that transcends ordinary sightseeing. You’ll not only witness the world but also feel it deeply within you. I hope to help you create memories that linger long after the journey ends.
