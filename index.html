My Journey as a Tour Guide

About Me

Dear Reader,

I’m Natig, or Frank if you prefer my English nickname. I've always had a thing for exploration, captivated by the beauty and complexity of the world. Seeing each wonder in awe, I want to share this feeling with others. I find solace in being a giant with shoulders for people to sit on, just as Newton once said (clickable).

How It All Began

My adventure began with a trip to Georgia when I was a kid. The rich history and warmth of the beach breeze stirred something deep within me in the city of Batumi. I was feeling a void in my life before discovering my actual purpose. Ever since becoming a guide, I’ve been on a quest to share that same magic with others.

Starting out as a local guide was intimidating. However, with each tour, I found joy in sharing stories and insights. As of October 10, 2024, I’ve guided over 600 tourists from more than 20 countries (clickable). Each encounter has shaped me, and while I’ve shared countless smiles with tourists, I felt accomplished at the end of each day.

My Philosophy

Guiding isn’t just a job; it’s a commitment to creating connections between places and nature with visitors. Travel is about sharing experiences, embracing the unknown, and learning from one another. As we wander through serene landscapes or busy streets, I aim to help you feel the vibes of each place we explore during our trips, even after.

I take pride in tailoring each tour to fit your unique interests. Whether you seek history, nature, or a blend of both, my goal is to create an experience that resonates with your preferences.

As an adventurer, you will embark on a journey that transcends ordinary sightseeing. You’ll not only witness but also feel the splendor of travel deep within you. I hope to help you create memories that linger long after the journey ends. Thank you :).
